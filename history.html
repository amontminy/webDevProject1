History of the breed?

If you want to know where our golden friends game from look no further! To get to where our goldens started we need to head overseas to England in the early 1800's. We set our eyes on a Dudley Coutts Marjoribanks a dog breeder from an early age and a prevalent one at that. After passing by a golden dog on a walk one day it piqued his interest and inspired Dudley to breed our golden friend into prominance. And in a rather surprising story an undesirably colored hunting dog began its run towards capturing American hearts.
