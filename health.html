How do you care for them?

Here we will give a summary of the most common health issues with the breed and how we can best prevent them in our furry friend.

Dental Diseas

Dental disease can affect any dog but is especially prevalent in our golden friends. This is an easy problem to solve though! Simply keep your goldens teeth clean with regularity and their pearly whites will stay that way.

Infection

Infection though largely a chance, can be best prevented by vaccination and attention. If your dog doesnt look good make sure to take them to the vet!

Obesity

A significat problem for golden retrievers obesity can lead to hip problems, digestive disorders, back pain, and heart disease. All of this can be prevented with a regular, consistent, and regulated diet.

Parasites

Testing testing testing... Will prevent many of the headaches from the parasites of the world. Make sure to check your dog regularly for ticks, bites, and bumps. And if something seems wrong take them to the vet and make sure your dog isnt carrying any unwelcome companions.
