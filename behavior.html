So how do they act then?

Theres a reason that goldens are considered one of the best family dogs. They are happy, athletic, loving, and trustworthy. All it takes to keep them happy is a good dose of attention and a walk or two every day. Good with other pets, people, and children theres nobody this breed wont take a liking to.

In summary heres some defining traits

  • Large Breed
  • Cheerful and Happy
  • Forgiving and Loving
  • Eager to please

If these sound like traits you'd want in your dog, youve found the right breed!
